Receiving feedback

Everyone will get a lot of feedback

Through these new feedback routines everyone will get much more feedback than at the moment. Everyone can now easily follow his own development.

Learn more

From rarely to regularly

At the moment in most companies people get feedback just rarely. Now everyone will get a small feedback weekly. So with just a quick look on the mobile, everyone can easily check how am I doing at the moment, what do my colleagues think about my contribution.

Short term and longterm development

The vertical bars show the average of the latest feedbacks, so basically how people are doing at the moment. The percentage value next to the bars describes the change. The horizontal bars describe the longterm average development.

A lot of compliments and improvement ideas

Everyone will get a lot of comments. Comments are divided in two categories: compliments and improvement ideas. What went well and how to improve. By sliding right users reply a comment with ‘I try to improve.’

Summary

Everyone will get much more feedback than at the moment. A lot of happy to hear moments but for sure also plenty wake up moments. No need for anyone to speculate anymore what do my colleagues think about my contribution. Just a quick look at your phone and you know it.
